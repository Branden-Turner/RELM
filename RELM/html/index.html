Research and Evidence Literacy in Medicine

 ...

 

This collaborative project, between the UW Health Sciences Library and the UW School of Medicine (UWSOM), to create online continuing medical education (CME) designed to develop research literacy skills in practicing physicians in the WWAMI program (Washington, Wyoming, Alaska, Montana, Idaho) who work with University of Washington School of Medicine students and residents.

Research and Evidence Literacy in Medicine encourages physicians to correctly apply the standards of evidence-based medicine when working with WWAMI students and residents and their own patients. The goal is to develop stronger and more congruent information skills among physicians practicing in rural and urban hospitals and clinics in the WWAMI states, who then model research literacy skills for the next generation of physicians.
